Instructions

These are going to be the instructions for participants.

The elements that need to be explained without the terminology are:

  1. Time Constraints:
    • Deadline: Get the higher score multiplier of 2x at 5 sec and then it will decrease until 10 sec with a 1x multiplier
    • Target: Get a score multiplier if you finish your movements at 10 sec.
  2. Workflow:
    • Leader-Follower:
      1. Leader: If you are designated as the leader you should create creative movements along the space that you have.
      2. Follower: If you are designated as the follower you should follow to the best of your capacities the leader of your team.
